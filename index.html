WHAT IS SUSTAINABLE LIVING?

Sustainable living is a lifestyle that attempts to reduce an individual's or society's use of the Earth's natural resources and personal resources. Practitioners of sustainable living often attempt to reduce their carbon footprint by altering methods of transportation, energy consumption, and diet. Proponents of sustainable living aim to conduct their lives in ways that are consistent with sustainability, in natural balance and respectful of humanity's symbiotic relationship with the Earth's natural ecology and cycles. The practice and general philosophy of sustainable living is highly interrelated with the overall principles of sustainable development.

The term sustainable living is predominantly used and defined in the context of environmentalism, and is broadly used to describe a lifestyle that is in balance with the natural environment and fostering a greater sense of environmental responsibility. Sustainable living is fundamentally the application of sustainability to lifestyle choice and decisions.

HOW TO START SUSTAINABLE LIVING

There are many ways to start living a more sustainable lifestyle. Here are a few tips to help you get started:

  1. Reduce waste by recycling and composting.
  2. Use reusable bags, bottles, and containers.
  3. Conserve water and energy.
  4. Support local and sustainable products.
  5. Educate others about sustainable living.

How to Start Sustainable Living in Shopping and Transportation

Shopping

Buy less: The most sustainable thing you can do is to consume less. Before making a purchase, ask yourself if you really need it.

Choose reusable over disposable items.

Support local and eco-friendly brands.

Transportation

Walk, bike, or take public transportation: These are the most sustainable ways to get around.

Carpool or share rides to reduce emissions.

Consider electric or hybrid vehicles.
